EFBC prefix to all,
A0 = A
90 = 0
41 = a

00 marks end of a name

names can be extended to max of 5 chars, or to the beginning of the next tag - 00, whichever comes first

90172E20 = Top Tag in list, you can use this to find values of other things 
Prefix for nametag is FF instead of EFBC, so EFBC = FFBC
e.g. make top tag say AaBb will store FF21FF41FF22FF42, meaning A is also 21, and a is 41

You need at least 8 zeroes after a name to be able to extend it by one letter, or 14 to extend two letters (max 5 letter names)

Edits are either 04 codes to Memory Loc or module edits at modloc in Section[5] of Sora_menu_name.rel

lowercase and some special characters use EFBD instead of EFBC, trial and error yay
