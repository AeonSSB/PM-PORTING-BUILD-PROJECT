Note: This render has costume specific colors on the fur materials. These are based on the FurEnv materials. 

If you go into LightChannel0, and then AmbientColor, you can see the material color for a specific costume.

For Sega Kid those colors are

Default: 7C87DB
Red: D08EC6
Green: ADC190
Blue: C1C7E1
White: C7C4CF

The render currently has set the color to: 7C87DB

Each object material that has fur in the blend has a Soft Light mix, and you need to use the appropriate color from the pac material. That include both head fur materials, the quill and tail, plus the lower leg material
